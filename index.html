ABOUT ME

Hello and welcome to Rainbow Way! I’m Alice.

Since I was a child, I have liked to match my clothes, and I am eager to engage in the fashion industry one day.I often hear my friends complain that they don’t know how to match the colour or some friends who avoid colour matching and wear black all the time.

Pantone publishes popular colours every year. These popular colours profoundly impact various industries, especially the fashion industry. If you know how to match the colours of clothes, you will catch the trend.

My mission is: to bring colour into every reader’s wardrobe.

My original intention was to create a blog to help people discover colour matching. 

At the same time, I am also a learner who wants to learn all aspects of fashion. 

Therefore, my blog is both sharing and a learning area. 

Anyone is very welcome to express their fashion views!
